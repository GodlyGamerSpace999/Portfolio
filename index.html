About me

I love reading lots of books, but mostly fantasy and mystery. My favorite authors are J.K. Rowling, Rick Riordan, and Shannon Messenger. I enjoy playing video games on my PS4 and computer. Some of my favorite games are Minecraft, Spiderman, and Horizon Zero Dawn. I like watching gaming videos by DanTDM, Mr. Beast, Dream, Loverfella, and Skip the Tutorial. Recently, I've been interested in cryptocurrency and the stock market. One of my favorite cryptocurrencies is the meme currency, Dogecoin. I moved to the U.S. when I was four and a half years old and I've had lots of challenges and experiences. I want to use this platform to record my achievements and share my progress with friends and family.
